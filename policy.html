Privacy policy

This blog is all about food: recipes, cooking tips, and general information about food and nutrition. We believe that good food should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget or cooking skills. We also believe in the power of home cooked meals, and the importance of cooking together as a family.

The following are our policies for the cooking blog. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

  1. All recipes must be original and created by the author. Recipes cannot be copied from another source.
  2. All recipes must be tested by the author before being published.
  3. All recipes must include clear and concise instructions.
  4. All recipes must be accompanied by photos.
  5. Authors must disclose any conflicts of interest, such as if they are being paid by a company to promote a product.
  6. The cooking blog reserves the right to edit any submitted recipes for clarity, grammar, and length.
  7. The cooking blog is not responsible for any errors or omissions in recipes.
  8. The cooking blog reserves the right to delete any comments that are deemed inappropriate, offensive, or spam.
  9. By submitting a recipe to the cooking blog, you are giving us permission to publish it on our website and share it on our social media channels.

This blog accepts forms of cash advertising, sponsorship, paid insertions or other forms of compensation.

The compensation received will never influence the content, topics or posts made in this blog. All advertising is in the form of advertisements generated by a third party ad network. Those advertisements will be identified as paid advertisements.

The owner(s) of this blog is compensated to provide opinion on products, services, websites and various other topics. Even though the owner(s) of this blog receives compensation for our posts or advertisements, we always give our honest opinions, findings, beliefs, or experiences on those topics or products. The views and opinions expressed on this blog are purely the bloggers' own. Any product claim, statistic, quote or other representation about a product or service should be verified with the manufacturer, provider or party in question.

This blog does not contain any content which might present a conflict of interest. For questions about this blog, please contact me.
