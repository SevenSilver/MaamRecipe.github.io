About Me

My name is Emily Carey, and I am passionate about cooking. I love trying new recipes and experimenting with different ingredients. I also enjoy sharing my recipes with others.

I started this blog as a way to share my love of cooking with others. I hope that my recipes will inspire others to get in the kitchen and experiment with new recipes of their own.

I grew up in a family of great cooks, and I have always loved spending time in the kitchen. I learned a lot from my family, and I continue to learn new things every day.

I currently live in Arizona State, and I love to cook for my friends and family. I hope that you will enjoy my recipes and that they will inspire you to get in the kitchen and cook something delicious!
