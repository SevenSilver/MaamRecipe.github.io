Repid Healthy Smoothies

Today we're going to prepare 4 extremely tasty smoothie bowls for a healthy and filling breakfast option.

Desert Healthy
Emily Carey

08 Oct, 2022 6:00 pm

But before moving onto the recipes, let's find out
Q. what a smoothie bowl is?
Ans. It's like a thick & creamy shake made by blending fruits, nut milks, and dry fruits. which we can enjoy in a bowl with different toppings. Want to know the truth? It tastes just like ice-cream but without sugar, artificial colours, cream or milk.

First, we're going to prepare a Kesar Pista smoothie bowl rich in flavours It tastes just like kulfi. After that we're going to prepare Instagram-perfect, super beautiful Jamun smoothie bowl. Then we're going to prepare a Caramel smoothie bowl rich with crunch of granola and finally, my personal favourite - a juicy Mango smoothie bowl with 3 ingredients.

All recipes in this video can serve 1 person. Come let's start with our Kesar Pista smoothie bowl

Kesar Pista

Ingredients

2 frozen bananas

10 pistachios, soaked

½ cup coconut milk

1 tbsp jaggery

2 dates, de-seeded

½ tsp cardamom powder

¼ tsp saffron strands, soaked in warm water/p>

pinch of rock salt

1 tbsp roasted and crushed pistachios

1 tbsp roasted and crushed almonds

1 tsp rose petals

few strands of saffron

Direction

Step 1

Most smoothie bowls require us to use frozen bananas. It's very important to use ripened bananas for this.

Q. What are the signs of ripened bananas?
Ans. They have small black dots all over them.
Solution. Most of the bananas we get from the market aren't properly ripened. If you keep them in a closet for 2-3 days, they will automatically ripen and show dots

Step 2

Then simply cut the banana into 5 pieces. keep them in a plate & place it in the freezer for at least 4-6 hours.

Q. You may be thinking - why are we using a freezer being Satvic?
Ans. So that you can get the thick & creamy texture in your smoothie.

Step 3

Along with this, you need 1/4 tsp saffron strands. which we had soaked in 1 tbsp warm water. By soaking the saffron in warm water for some time, its flavour comes out better. Just add 2 frozen bananas in your mixer & 10 pistachios soaked in water for 6 hours. Add them along with the water & a pinch of rock salt. Now just switch on the blender and blend everything together well. There - our smoothie is ready! Take it out in a bowl.

Q. Did you know what the most fun part of making a smoothie bowl is?
Ans. Making its toppings Whenever you want to make something special for breakfast for friends/family this is the perfect option. They won't even know if they're having a smoothie or dessert.

Jamun smoothie bowl

Now it's time for our next smoothie bowl. But whenever I make this smoothie bowl, I spent 30 mins clicking pictures of it. This is our vibrant and colourful Jamun smoothie bowl.

Ingredients

1 kg whole jaamun or 1½ cups de-seeded jaamun

2 frozen bananas

10 soaked almonds

3 dates, de-seeded

½ cup water

Direction

Step 1

You need approximately 1/2 kg Jamun to make this. Keep in mind to buy thick fleshy jamuns, Buy dark jamuns because they are sweet.

Step 2

Now with a knife, cut the seeds out and take out the flesh in a bowl. I know it takes a little more time to make this smoothie bowl. We usually make this when a family member or guest is coming over.

Step 3

After that, add all these de-seeded jamuns, 2 frozen bananas. Blend everything well until you get a vibrant purple colour.

Ta-da! Your vibrant and beautiful smoothie bowls are ready.
You can add your favourite fruits, nuts or seeds to it if you want. I will add yellow mangoes to it and some seeds.

Caramel smoothie bowl

Let's move onto our third smoothie bowl - Caramel smoothie bowl. When I tasted this smoothie bowl for the first time, I thought I was having caramel ice-cream.

Ingredients

5 soaked cashews

5 soaked almonds

3 soaked walnuts

1 tsp jaggery

3 dates, de-seeded

½ tsp cinnamon powder

1/4 tsp rock salt

1/3 cup water

2 frozen bananas

1 tbsp raisins

1 tsp watermelon seeds

1 tsp sunflower seeds

1 tsp chia seeds

Direction

Step 1

Take a medium mixer jar and We have soaked all 3 of them in water for 6 hours. It's very important to soak nuts before using them that's it. Switch on your blender Then add 2 frozen bananas to it and blend it again and now our tasty and creamy caramel smoothie bowl is ready. But the real flavour in this recipe will be added by our homemade crunchy granola.

Step 2

To make it, place a pan on low flame. Now roast them all for 2-3 mins lightly. Be careful that they don't get burnt. Or else, they'll taste bitter. There! Our granola is ready!

Step 3

Now take out your smoothie in a bowl and add granola with your favourite fruits. We're adding chickoo and banana to it

Ta-da! beautiful smoothie bowls are ready.

Mango Smoothie Bowl

Come let's move onto our last recipe - my family favourite summer special made with juicy & sweet mangoes - mango smoothie bowl. This is the easiest recipe to make out of all the 4 smoothie bowls.

Ingredients

10 soaked almonds

1/4 cup water

1 large mango or 2 medium sized mangoes, put in the fridge for about 20 minutes

any fruits, nuts & seeds

Direction

Step 1

To make this, you need a big ripe mango or 2 medium sized mangoes. My personal favourite is alphonso mango. Ah! There's something special about just the aroma of mangoes, Keep the mango in the fridge for at least 20 mins to cool. Be mindful to keep them in the fridge and not the freezer.

Step 2

When your mango has cooled down, peel it and cut it into thick pieces. Don't stop until your clothes get dirty and your hands are soaked with its juice. Oh! I was only kidding! and blend it again. Now our cool, juicy and refreshing mango smoothie is ready!

Step 3

Take it out in a bowl and top it with your favourite fruit.

Ta-da! Your smoothie bowls are ready.
If you wish, you can pack these smoothie bowls in a tiffin to take to your office.

FAQ

Q. how many frequently can I consume smoothie bowls?

Ans. If you're in the healing stage, then eating once a week is enough because they're a little heavy And if you're in the lifestyle stage with no health problems, then you can eat them twice or thrice a week and children and athlete can consume them everyday too, no problem.
